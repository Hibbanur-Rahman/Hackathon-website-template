What is Coding Carnival?

Coding Carnival is 24 hour long running hackathon will be held on February 15th to 18th , We calls for hackers, artists, coders, designers, tech evangelists, creatives, and developers from all disciplines to take a part to win awesome prizes and collaborate with other developers . Throughout the weekend hackers at Coding Carnival will have unique opportunities to learn from each other trying hands in new technologies We are determined to have an all-inclusive and diverse group of students attending.

What is a Code-a-thon?
A codeathon is a 24 hour event where "coders" all over the globe will collaborate on creating a functioning software and hardware by the end of the event.

Who can attend?
Coding Carnival is open to all undergraduate, high school students and middle school students from all schools.

I am a first time hacker, what should i do?
No worries, we love to welcome first time hackers and start their hacking journey from a hackathon. We have some awesome workshops and events which will help you get started with hackathon and hacking in general.

How team formation works?
You can form team with 4 members in it at maximum. If you don't have a team, there'll be plenty of opportunities for you to meet hackers and form a team!

How to register myself in the codeathon?
All you need is to fill our form above and join Discord, we will guide you through everything there.

I have more questions?
Reach us directly at (rehan@limbohacks.tech or hello@limbohacks.tech) we would happy to help you.

Can i volunteer?
Yes we are actively looking for volunteers, fill the form (in footer section) we will be glad to have you.

Will there be swag?
Yes! Participants will recieveing Limbo Hacks swag!.
